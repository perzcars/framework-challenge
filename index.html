Frequently Asked Questions

Whether you are a boba conoisseur or entirely new to the drink, at Boba Bros, we want you to have an easy and delicious experience every time! Here is a collection of the most frequently asked questions.

What is Boba?

Boba is a Taiwanese drink consisting of tea (usually cold and milk-based) as well as chewy tapioca pearls. Originating in the early 1980s, boba tea has since spread to each corner of the world and is enjoyed by many for its delicious taste and customizable options.

What makes your boba special?

Our boba shop uses imported Taiwanese tapioca pearls for as close to an authentic taste as possible. We also are constantly listening to customer reviews and feedback; we want to provide you with the best boba in Michigan, so we are always receptive to you!

What types of boba tea do you sell?

SWEETNESS LEVEL 0% sweet, 25% sweet, 50% sweet, 100% sweet
EXTRA TOPPINGS Extra tapioca, grass jelly, red bean
